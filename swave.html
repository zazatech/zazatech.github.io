S-Wave : Make your smart device talk to smartphone

S-Wave is a tiny modem that allows devices to wirelessly communicate with a smartphone using inaudible sound waves.

Unique Features

Ubiquitous Connectivity

S-Wave leverages existing hardware for sound available in the smartphone to allow devices to communicate with every kind of smartphone available.There is no additional hardware needed in the smartphone.

Simple to use

S-Wave enables users to connect and communicate with devices without having to pair or change any settings in their smartphone.

Easy to integrate

S-Wave Solution provides digital I/O interface & application SDK for devices and smartphone integration.
